Activities

Historical Sites

Discover Fayetteville's rich historical background, including its Civil War and Coal Mining history.

Rafting

The New River runs right through Fayetteville. Go whitewater rafting with our experienced raft guides!

Hiking

You will never run out of trails in the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve.

Biking

Fayetteville is home to many hiking/biking trails. Get with out friends at Arrowhead Bike Farm and start cycling!

Kayaking

Want to challenge your kayaking skills, or do you want to just float along? We have all the good spots!

Swimming

You can take a dip in the river, or try out Ace Adventure Waterpark for a one of a kind experience!
